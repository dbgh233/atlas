## Pipeline Stage Definitions

| Stage | Owner | Definition | Exit Criteria |
|-------|-------|-----------|---------------|
| Discovery 30% | Sales | Lead created in GHL. Discovery is booked OR in progress. No commitment yet. | Discovery call completed with clear outcome: Onboarding Scheduled (goal), NO, or follow-up booked. |
| Committed 60% | Sales | Discovery completed. Merchant gave verbal YES. Onboarding not yet scheduled due to specific reason. Next step must have timeline and correlating task. | Onboarding scheduled. |
| Onboarding Scheduled 70% | Onboarding | Onboarding is booked on calendar. Waiting for call to occur. | Onboarding completed. |
| MPA & Underwriting 80% | Onboarding | Signed MPA + all supporting docs submitted to processor. Ball is with the bank. | Processor approval received, all conditions/stips cleared. |
| Approved 90% | Sales/CS | Processor approved. All conditions cleared. Ready for integration. A contingent approval is NOT an approval. | Integration call completed, gateway/terminal live, test batch successful. |
| Live 100% | CS | Merchant is actively processing. | $$$ |
| Close Lost 0% | Sales | Deal is dead. Reason must be documented. | -- |

## Pipeline SLAs (Discovery -> Live)

**SLA Note:** SLAs represent the ideal timeline when nothing goes wrong. Exceeding an SLA is a signal, not a failure. When a deal exceeds SLA, document why in GHL notes. The reason surfaces in Monday Pipeline Triage for decision-making. The goal is visibility, not punishment.

| Stage Transition | SLA | Owner | What This Means |
|-----------------|-----|-------|-----------------|
| Discovery -> Committed | On call | Sales | Get a YES or NO on the call. If "maybe," BAMFAM a follow-up. No open-ended maybes. |
| Committed -> Onboarding Scheduled | 48 hours | Sales | 48 hours from commitment or Discovery call. Use Meet with Henry calendar for follow-ups (NOT Discovery link). |
| Onboarding -> MPA & Underwriting | 48 hours (72hr escalation) | Onboarding | 48 hours from when onboarding call ENDS. Ideal: MPA signed + submitted same day. No partial submissions. |
| MPA & Underwriting -> Approved | 120 hours (5 biz days) | Onboarding | Follow up with bank every 24 hours (business days). Respond to notes SAME DAY. Contingent approvals remain in MPA stage. |
| Approved -> Live | 168 hours (7 days) | Sales/CS | Book integration call within 48 hours of approval. Gateway setup + test batch within 7 days. |

## Lead Cadences (Before Discovery is Booked)

| Lead Source | Speed to Contact | Cadence | If Exhausted |
|------------|-----------------|---------|--------------|
| Referral Partners | Day 0: Email + call | Double-dial + text Day 0. Call x2 Days 1-2. Call x1 Days 3-5. Book Discovery in 3 biz days. | Tag Cold Inactive |
| Merchant Referrals / Inbound | Within 24 hours (HIGHEST priority) | Double-dial + text Day 0. Call Days 1-2. Call + email Days 2, 4, 7. 5 calls, 3 emails, 2 SMS over 7 days. | Tag Cold Inactive |
| Events / Associations | At event: book on spot. Post: 1-day buffer then outreach within 3 days. | Day 1: Email + call. Day 2: Call + SMS. Day 6: Call + email. Day 11: Final. 4 calls, 3 emails, 2 SMS over 11 days. | Tag Cold Inactive |
| No-Show (booked, didn't show) | Same day: SMS + email | Same-day text + email. Call Days 1, 3, 5. 3 calls, 2 emails, 1 SMS over 5 biz days. | Tag Cold Inactive |

## Critical Rules

1. **BAMFAM is not optional.** Never end a call without the next step booked.
2. **No partial submissions.** Do NOT submit until ALL required documents are collected.
3. **Same-day response to underwriting notes.** If received late (Pacific bank), first thing next morning.
4. **Approval Date auto-stamps** when opp moves to Approved. Onboarding moves opp only when ALL conditions/stips are cleared.
5. **Onboarding must mark Appointment Status = "Completed"** after every onboarding call. This stamps Onboarding Completed Date and starts submission SLA.
6. **No stage skipping.** Each stage's requirements must be met before moving forward.
7. **Every opportunity gets worked to Live or explicitly Closed.** No quiet deaths.

---

## Daily Rules

1. Work your GHL task list FIRST every morning. Lead tasks -> Committed tasks -> Pipeline -> Hunt.
2. Never end a call without the next step booked. BAMFAM is not optional.
3. If a stage exceeds its SLA, escalate to appropriate party and then Drew in weekly Pipeline Triage (Mondays). Do not let it sit.
4. Double-dial every first call attempt. Front-load reach-outs. Volume negates luck.
5. Respond to underwriting notes SAME DAY. If received late (Pacific bank), first thing next morning.
